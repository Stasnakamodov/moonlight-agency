# AI Integration for Business

Artificial intelligence is no longer just a trend — it's a necessary tool for competitive business.

## Popular Scenarios

1. **AI Chatbots** — customer support automation
2. **Document Processing** — OCR + LLM for data extraction
3. **Recommendation Systems** — user personalization
4. **Predictive Analytics** — trend forecasting

## Model Selection

Different tasks require different models:

- **GPT-4** — complex text tasks
- **Claude** — document and code analysis
- **Local models** — when data privacy matters

## Telegram Integration

AI bots in Telegram are one of the most effective business channels. They're available 24/7 and don't require app installation.

## Conclusion

Start with a simple scenario and gradually expand AI usage. We'll help you choose the optimal approach for your business.
